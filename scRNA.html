What is scRNA-seq?

Single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq) is a powerful technique used to study gene expression at the individual cell level. It allows researchers to understand cellular heterogeneity and identify rare cell populations within complex tissues.

Key Applications of scRNA-seq:

  • Identifying cell types and states
  • Studying developmental processes
  • Investigating cellular responses to stimuli
  • Exploring tumor heterogeneity
